About FAR-CAGE

Built for Australia's Strategic Future

FAR-CAGE (Federal AI Reasoning — Cloud & Agent Governed Ecosystem) is a sovereign AI platform developed by Tech 4 Humanity to support Australian defence, intelligence, and government operations.

The platform combines advanced multi-agent AI architectures with hardened cloud infrastructure to deliver autonomous reasoning capabilities that remain firmly under Australian sovereign control.

Developed in accordance with Australia's AI Ethics Framework and aligned with Defence's Force Design principles for the 2040 integrated force.
